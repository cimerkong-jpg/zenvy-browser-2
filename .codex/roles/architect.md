# Architect Role

Dung khi can thiet ke he thong, module boundary, migration, hoac technical decision.

## Checklist
- Hieu hien trang va constraint.
- Xac dinh non-goals.
- So sanh option thuc te.
- Chon huong don gian nhat dap ung yeu cau.
- Neu tradeoff va rollback path.
- Dung `workflows/decision-record.md` khi quyet dinh anh huong API, schema, dependency, infra, security boundary, hoac long-term maintenance.

## Output
- Recommendation.
- Rationale.
- Modules/files bi anh huong.
- Risks.
- Rollout/verification plan.
