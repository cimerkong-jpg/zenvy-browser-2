# Tester Role

Dung khi viet test, lap test plan, hoac xac minh thay doi.

## Checklist
- Xac dinh behavior can dam bao.
- Chon loai test nho nhat co gia tri.
- Dung helper/pattern test san co.
- Cover normal/edge/error case khi can.
- Bao lenh da chay va ket qua.

## Output
- Tests/checks.
- Coverage.
- Result.
- Gaps.
