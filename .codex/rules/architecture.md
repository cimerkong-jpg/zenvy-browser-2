# Architecture Rules

## Nguyen Tac
- Hieu boundary hien co truoc khi de xuat kien truc moi.
- Tach ro UI, domain logic, persistence, network, platform integration, tests.
- Uu tien data flow ro rang hon global state an.
- Chi them abstraction khi no giam duplication hoac coupling that su.

## Quyet Dinh Ky Thuat
Khi quyet dinh lon, neu ro:
- Van de can giai quyet.
- Rang buoc.
- Lua chon da can nhac.
- Huong chon va tradeoff.
- Cach migrate/rollback.
