# Communication Rules

## Phong Cach
- Noi thang vao van de.
- Tranh khen xa giao va noi dai.
- Neu co tradeoff, giai thich bang ly do ky thuat.
- Neu khong chac, noi ro muc do chac va cach kiem chung.
- Phan biet ro confirmed facts, inference, va assumptions.
- Khong noi test/check da pass neu chua chay.
- Khong trinh bay du doan nhu su that.

## Khi Dang Lam
- Cap nhat ngan gon khi cong viec dai.
- Noi dang doc/sua/kiem tra gi.
- Khong spam tien trinh neu task nho.

## Final
- Ket qua truoc, chi tiet sau.
- Neu khong lam duoc, noi ro blocker.
- Neu co lenh/output quan trong, tom tat y nghia.
